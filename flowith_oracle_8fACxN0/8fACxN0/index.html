Meet the Fennec Fox!

The cutest little fox from the desert with the BIGGEST ears you've ever seen!

Those Amazing Big Ears!

  • ‚òÖ Their enormous ears can grow up to 6 inches long - that's half the size of their body!
  • ‚òÖ These big ears help them hear insects and animals moving underground.
  • ‚òÖ The ears also work like air conditioners to keep them cool in the hot desert!

Amazing Desert Adaptations

Furry Feet

Their feet are covered with fur that protects them from hot sand and helps them walk without sinking!

Sandy Camouflage

Their sandy-colored fur helps them blend in perfectly with the desert environment!

Water Savers

Their kidneys are specially adapted to conserve water, and they can go long periods without drinking!

Night Owls

They're nocturnal, which means they sleep during the hot day and come out at night when it's cooler!

Home & Food

Home Sweet Home: Underground Dens

  • ‚òÖ Fennec foxes dig deep tunnels and caves in the sand called dens.
  • ‚òÖ These dens can be 20 feet long with many rooms and exits!
  • ‚òÖ Underground homes keep them cool during the day and warm at night.

What's for Dinner?

  • ‚òÖ Fennec foxes eat both plants and animals - they're omnivores!
  • ‚òÖ Their favorite foods include insects, small rodents, lizards, and birds.
  • ‚òÖ They also enjoy desert fruits, leaves, and roots.
  • ‚òÖ They get most of their water from the food they eat!

Family Life

  • ‚òÖ Fennec foxes live in family groups of up to 10 members!
  • ‚òÖ Baby fennec foxes are called kits.
  • ‚òÖ A mother fox usually has 2-5 kits at once.
  • ‚òÖ Both mom and dad take care of their babies!
  • ‚òÖ They communicate with purrs, whimpers, and barks - they can even scream!

Fun Facts About Fennec Foxes

1

Fennec foxes live about 10 years in the wild and up to 14 years in zoos!

2

3
