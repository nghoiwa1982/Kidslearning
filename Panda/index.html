Fun Panda Facts

Great Swimmers

Unlike what many people think, pandas are actually quite good swimmers!

Expert Climbers

Giant panda cubs learn how to climb trees at just 5 months old, sometimes practicing on their mothers!

Poop Champions

Pandas poop around 40 times a day! That's because bamboo is hard to digest and moves through their bodies quickly.

Camouflage Masters

Giant panda's black and white markings help them hide in their habitat. The white helps them hide in snow, and the black helps them hide in shade.

Learn to Draw a Panda

Would you like to draw your own panda? Follow these simple steps:

  1. Draw a big circle for the head
  2. Add a smaller oval below for the body
  3. Draw two circles for ears on top of the head
  4. Add two black patches around the eyes
  5. Draw a small nose and mouth
  6. Add arms, legs, and a small tail
  7. Color the ears, eye patches, arms, and legs black
  8. The rest stays white - and you have a panda!

Remember, practice makes perfect! Don't worry if your first panda doesn't look perfect.
